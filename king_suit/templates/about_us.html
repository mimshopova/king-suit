About Us

There is a large selection of suits, shirts, ties, bow ties, belts, and shoes in our stores. With this in mind, we work hard to fulfill all of your requests and suggestions for the upcoming event. Our edge over competing companies is the wide selection of colors, models, and cuts for every man. The truth is that everyone appreciates attractive individuals who know how to groom themselves and present themselves well. This is why investing in a high-quality, designer men's suit will benefit your overall appearance and sense of style. We provide good quality at competitive pricing because of this. The materials are either viscose and wool or 100% cold wool. Our models follow the current season's global fashion trends, which include checks, dots, and fabrics with structural patterns. Only in stores will you find the full range of graduation attire and accessories.

History

"King Suit" is a brand that was founded 13 years ago. We first introduced the western fashion of fitted suits in 2008, when baggy suits were still popular. That is why we chose the motto "Always a step ahead of the competition." to describe ourselves. Every year, the company's team works hard to present new and stylish trends that will make you stand out. We create our own models for you that you will not find in other stores. They are created with taste and quality in mind. We are proud that one of our previous models was also sewn for the global market by large factories and was a success.

Philosophy

The "King Suit" philosophy is impeccable stitching and a rich color palette, so our collections are a mix of color contrasts in sports and formal styles. We offer you a modern approach to dressing with them.

Why us?

We offer discounts on complete suits, shirts, ties or bow ties, belts, and shoes. We have two stores in Plovdiv with over 200 different suit models. Aside from our official collection, we also have a sporty-elegant one for graduation parties, wedding guests, various events, or just everyday wear. High-quality items at reasonable prices for all current and prospective customers.
